Media Manager

To play audio, you should upload media files to the decoder. Only .wav, .mp3 and .aac files are supported. After the file is uploaded, go to "Add State" or open a state to edit above, click on "Add Value" and select in "Name on a decoder" dropdown "Speakers" and you should see all uploaded files in the "Value" dropdown.
